# ARTICLE VI. BUILDING AND STORAGE NEAR VIADUCTS RESTRICTED

## Sec. 10-101. Definitions.

The following words and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings respectively ascribed to them:

Material shall mean any inflammable, or explosive substance, liquid, gas or anything which supports fire.

Viaduct shall also include approaches to viaducts.

*((Code 1950, § 378.1))*

Cross reference(s)---Definitions and rules of construction generally, § 1-2.

## Sec. 10-102. Measurement.

The distances referred to in this article shall be the horizontal distance measured from a line projected perpendicularly from the outermost part at the nearest point of the viaduct.

*((Code 1950, § 378.2))*

## Sec. 10-103. Structures near viaducts.

(a)	No building or structure shall be erected within twenty (20) feet of a viaduct unless of type I construction throughout, but in no event less than ten (10) feet from a viaduct.

(b)	Nothing in this section shall prohibit the erection or maintenance of loading docks and passenger platforms under or within twenty (20) feet of a viaduct if of type I construction.

*((Code 1950, § 378.3))*

## Sec. 10-104. Skylights near viaducts.

No skylight shall hereafter be installed within twenty (20) feet of a viaduct on any existing building or any building hereafter erected when the roof of such building is below the floor level of a viaduct.

*((Code 1950, § 378.4))*

## Sec. 10-105. Window openings near viaducts.

Hereafter no window openings shall be installed in a wall in any existing building or any building hereafter erected below the floor level of a viaduct when such wall faces and is within twenty (20) feet of a viaduct, unless the sash of such windows is of steel and glazed with wire glass and unless such window openings are equipped with automatic sprinklers installed in an approved manner.

*((Code 1950, § 378.5))*

## Sec. 10-106. Storage of goods near viaducts.

It shall be unlawful to maintain, deposit, store, or otherwise place material under or within a distance of twenty (20) feet of any part of a viaduct unless contained in a type I, II, or III building as defined in the building code of the city, provided, that nothing contained in this article shall prohibit the following under or within twenty-nine (29) feet of a viaduct:

    (1)	The moving, loading, unloading, placing, or standing of railroad cars, locomotives, or equipment, in the course of ordinary railroad operations or traffic, regardless of contents or lading; or

    (2)	The moving, loading, unloading, placement, or delivery to or transfer from places of business of goods, materials, or produce in the ordinary course of manufacture, trade, or commerce; or

    (3)	The parking or temporary storage of automobiles or other vehicles; but such parking shall be within the time limits fixed by other applicable Code provisions or ordinances.

*((Code 1950, § 378.6))*
