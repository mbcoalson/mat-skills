## Form Section: Description of Improvements and Upgrades

**Prompt:**
> Provide a list of activities or measures needed to be taken to complete the Energize Denver performance requirements for buildings 5,000 to 24,999 sq ft.

**Example response:**

**EXAMPLE OF LED LIGHTING COMPLIANCE PLAN**

The proposed retrofit involves upgrading all interior and exterior lighting systems from existing fluorescent, incandescent, and metal halide fixtures to high-efficiency LED lighting throughout the building.

Scope of Work:
- Replace approximately 60 interior fluorescent T8 fixtures with equivalent LED fixtures
- Replace approximately 10 exterior metal halide wall packs and parking lot lights with LED fixtures
