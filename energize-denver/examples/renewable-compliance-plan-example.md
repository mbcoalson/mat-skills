# Example: Renewable Energy Compliance Plan (5,000–24,999 sq ft)

> **ILLUSTRATIVE ONLY.** All dates, system specs, and scope details below are example values from a CASR-published sample form. **Do not copy-paste any of this content verbatim into a client filing.** Replace every example value (dates, capacity, offset percentages, descriptions, selected checkboxes) with client-specific information. Note that the source example's timeline is not strictly sequential (see inline note below) — any reuse must establish a logically ordered project schedule.

> Markdown replication of the CASR "Compliance Plan" online form, populated with an example 20% solar pathway submission. Extracted from a PDF screenshot for context efficiency. Use as a structural reference when drafting a compliance plan for a client pursuing the renewable energy option under the small-building requirements.

## Form Section: Description of Improvements and Upgrades

**Prompt:**
> Provide a list of activities or measures needed to be taken to complete the Energize Denver performance requirements for buildings 5,000 to 24,999 sq ft.

**Example response:**

**EXAMPLE OF RENEWABLE ENERGY COMPLIANCE PLAN**

ABC Property Management, LLC, intends to install a rooftop 100 kW DC Solar Photovoltaic (PV) system to comply with Energize Denver Requirements.

System Details:
- System Type: Grid-tied rooftop solar PV
- Capacity: 100 kW DC
- Expected Annual Generation: Approx. 140,000 kWh
- Installation Location: Rooftop of 123 Main Street
- Installer: XYZ Renewables Inc.
- Estimated Offset: 28% of annual building electricity consumption

## Form Section: Timeline for Improvements and Upgrades

**Prompt:**
> Projected timeline of implementation that will account for the reasoning behind the timeline extension. This will be used to inform the new compliance deadline.

**Example timeline table:**

| # | Activity | Description | Proposed Implementation Date |
|---|----------|-------------|------------------------------|
| 1 | Site Evaluation & Solar Feasibility | Site visit, roof load analysis, shading study | October 2025 |
| 2 | Design Finalization & Permitting | Detailed design, electrical plans, permit submittal | November 2025 |
| 3 | Equipment Procurement | Purchase of solar panels, inverters | April 2025 |
| 4 | Installation | System installation, electrical interconnection | April 2026 |

> Note: "Equipment Procurement" in April 2025 precedes "Design Finalization" in November 2025 in the source PDF. This appears to be an inconsistency in the CASR example itself (or a data-entry artifact) rather than a planning error — preserved verbatim.

## When to Use This Example

Reference this when a client:
- Owns a building 5,000–24,999 sq ft subject to Energize Denver Sec. 10-405
- Is pursuing the **20% solar** compliance option (not LED lighting)
- Needs a template for the CASR compliance plan form fields

**Pathway requirement reminder:** The renewable option requires on-site or off-site solar generating **20% of the building's annual energy usage**. The example above targets ~28% offset, exceeding the minimum.

The structure mirrors the CASR online form. Client-specific content replaces the example text in System Details, Timeline table rows, Deadline selection, and Documentation checkboxes.
